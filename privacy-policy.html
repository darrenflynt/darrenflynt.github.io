PRIVACY POLICY.

Last Updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Darren Flynt ("I," "me," or "my") collects, uses, and handles information when you visit darrenflynt.com (the "Site"). I take your privacy seriously and am committed to being transparent about my practices.

10. CONTACT

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact form on this Site.
